Our Story

Huddle was born out of a real-life need to strengthen our relationship. When a product manager married a therapist, we thought we had the communication thing down. But a few weeks after having our first child, we realized we needed to step up our game. We found that monthly retrospectives were a effective way to check in, ensure we were supporting each other, and consciously grow our relationship.

This ritual became a dedicated time to set goals, hold ourselves accountable, validate each other's feelings, and be truly vulnerable. While we practice these habits throughout the month, having a focused time for reflection helps us truly tune into our relationship and ensure we are always on the same page.
